Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

EYE collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Public social media data: Publicly available profile information, follower counts, engagement statistics, and content metadata from TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram through their official APIs.
  • Account information: Email address and password for users who create an account on our platform.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display aggregated analytics and engagement metrics for content creators
  • Help brands and agencies evaluate potential influencer partnerships
  • Generate demographic estimates based on publicly available signals
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of our analytics

3. Data from Social Media Platforms

We access social media data exclusively through official APIs provided by each platform. We only access publicly available information and comply with each platform's API terms of service, rate limits, and data usage policies. We do not collect or store private messages, non-public content, or data from private accounts.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except as required by law or with explicit user consent. Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be shared with authorized platform users.

6. Data Retention

Public social media data is refreshed periodically and older data is automatically replaced. User account data is retained until the account is deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Content creators whose public data appears on our platform may request removal by contacting us.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@eyesocial.app
